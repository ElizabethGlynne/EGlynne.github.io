About My Teaching

My teaching emphasizes inclusive, student-centered biology instruction grounded in real organisms, evolutionary thinking, and hands-on learning. I integrate field experiences, laboratory investigation, and digital tools to help students build conceptual understanding and scientific confidence. My courses emphasize pattern recognition, comparative reasoning, and evidence-based inquiry.

Teaching Specialties

  • Introductory Biology (majors and non-majors)
  • Organismal Biology, Zoology, and Herpetology
  • Vertebrate Anatomy and Comparative Morphology
  • Ecology/Evolution-centered biology instruction
  • Field-based ecology and inquiry-driven labs
  • Digital anatomy and virtual dissection tools
